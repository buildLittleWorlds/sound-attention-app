What to notice

The ear does not receive a pitch in isolation for long. A surrounding chord can make it feel supported; a short phrase can make the same pitch feel like a destination.

Honest limit

This does not prove that music has one fixed meaning. It only gives you a small, repeatable way to hear how context can change the role a sound seems to play.
